Beauty Without Compromise

At Wehman’s Jewelry, we believe that the story behind your jewelry should be just as beautiful as the piece itself.

Conflict-Free Diamonds and Gemstones

Every diamond and gemstone in our collection is sourced through the Kimberley Process and other legitimately recognized channels. We ensure our supply chain is free from the human cost often associated with jewelry.

Recycled Metals

To reduce our environmental footprint, 50% of our common jewelry is crafted from certified recycled gold and silver, preventing unnecessary mining and waste.

Fair Labor

We work exclusively with workshops that provide living wages, safe conditions, and health benefits to the artisans who bring our designs to life.

15%

The Final Link

Our ethics don't stop at the workbench. For every piece sold, we return 15% of our profits to our neighbors facing homelessness, food insecurity, and poverty.
