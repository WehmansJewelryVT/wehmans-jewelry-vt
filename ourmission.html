Our Mission

"To make jewelry accessible for all and to support underdeveloped communities in the process."

From accessible essentials to heirloom treasures, we believe luxury should be inclusive. That’s why we pledge 15% of all profits to local charities combatting homelessness, food insecurity, and poverty. Also, all of our jewelry will range from a silver and semiprecious ring to higher end precious and more expensive rings. This will ensure that everyone is able to find the piece they're looking for, no matter their budget. There will be also be an exclusive collections page for those who are interested in truly high end one-of-a-kind pieces. Here at Wehman's Jewelry LLC, we belive that everyone should own a piece no matter their budget and that we can slowly but surely help local communities by donating profits to chairities. We will also have an additional charity for the buyers who are interested in sending money to other causes like global warming research or helping someone who is in a bad situation but can't pay for it. We hope you enoy your experience here at Wehman's Jewelry and we also hope to help everyone and treat everyone as family. There will also be translation services in our store for those who use to use it which will translate text into 150 different langauges as diversity and inclusion is also a key part fo our mission.
