The Wehman’s Legacy

Located in the heart of Burlington, VT, Wehman’s Jewelry is a new jewelry business specializing in jewelry sourced ethically and/or sustainably from all over the world. The hope is to sell jewelry at a wide range of price points while also helping the local community.
