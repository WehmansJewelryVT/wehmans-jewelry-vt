Expertise, Ethics, and Our 15% Promise

Our 15% Mission

Every purchase at Wehman’s makes an impact. We take 15% of our net profits and distribute them monthly to local charities focusing on homelessness, food insecurity, and poverty relief.

Not at all. We believe giving is a cost of doing business, not an extra fee for our customers. Our prices remain competitive because we prioritize community over maximum profit.
