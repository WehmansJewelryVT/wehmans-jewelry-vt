Our Story & Mission

A Dream Rooted in Burlington

Wehman’s Jewelry VT wasn't born out of a boardroom, but from a lifelong dream to blend the art of fine jewelry with the heart of our community. We believe that everyone deserves to celebrate life's most precious moments with something beautiful, regardless of their budget.

Our founder envisioned a different kind of jewelry store—one where transparency, craftsmanship, and local support are the foundation of everything we do.

Reasonable Pricing

High-end jewelry often comes with high-end markups. At Wehman's, we keep our overhead low and our prices fair, ensuring quality pieces are accessible to all our neighbors in Vermont.

Giving Back

We believe a business is only as strong as the community it serves. That’s why a portion of every single sale is donated to local charities to provide financial assistance to those in need.

Local Integrity

From custom designs to simple repairs, we treat every customer with the same respect and honesty. You aren't just a client; you're a part of the Burlington community we love.

"Our goal is simple: to make the world a little brighter, one piece of jewelry and one local act of kindness at a time."
