About The Owner

Wehman's Jewelry VT was founded by Nathan Wehman, a person who had a passion in gemstones and minerals and also wanted to make a positive impact on the world. Nathan Wehman started this business at age 25 after graduting from the GIA Colored Gemstone Identification class and graduating from CCV with his Associate's in Business.

Reasonable Pricing

High-end jewelry often comes with high-end markups. At Wehman's Jewelry, we believe that everyone should be able to celebrate that special moment without breeaking the bank. We have a wide vareity of jewelry designs and options that are ranging from inexpensive like .925 silver amethyst or other semi-precious stone settings to expensive jewelry like a 14K gold ring with a high quality ruby.

Giving Back

We believe a business is only as strong as the community it serves. That’s why a portion of our profits are donated to local charities to provide financial assistance to those in need. We aim to do this by having the 15% rule which means that for every profit we make on jewelry sales, 15% of those profits go to local chairities like foodlessness, homelessness, and poverty.

Local Integrity

From custom designs to simple repairs, and everything in between, we treat every customer with the same respect and honesty. You aren't just a customer; you're a part of the Burlington community we love.
